Step by Step

The Build Process

We pride ourselves on transparent communication, ensuring you are informed and comfortable at every stage of the construction.

1

Consultation & Planning

We review your architectural drawings, assess the site constraints, and provide a comprehensive, fully itemized quotation.

2

Groundworks & Footings

The foundation of quality. We handle excavation, drainage alterations, and pouring the concrete footings to building control standards.

3

Structural Shell

Erecting the blockwork and brickwork, installing steels (RSJs) to create open spaces, and constructing the pitched or flat roof.

4

First & Second Fix

Coordinating plumbing and electrics, followed by plastering, bespoke joinery (skirting, doors), and final handover.
