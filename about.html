Our Journey

Crafting Excellence Since 2020. Backed by 18 Years of Mastery.

At Tom Cutts Joinery and Building, we don't just build structures; we craft environments where people live, work, and thrive. Founded officially in 2020, our company was built upon a foundation of deep industry knowledge, driven by our Managing Director, Tom Cutts.

With over 18 years of hands-on and managerial experience in the construction sector, Tom and the team have perfected their craft across all scales of work. From intricate bespoke joinery and flawless kitchen installations to large-scale commercial developments and heritage restorations, our diverse portfolio speaks for itself.

We are incredibly proud to serve our local communities throughout Colne, Burnley, Pendle, the Ribble Valley, and the wider North West. Our philosophy is simple: unparalleled quality, transparent pricing, and a commitment to ensuring every single client is 100% satisfied before we leave the site.
