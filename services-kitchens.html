Our Expertise

Complete Fit-Out Services

From domestic heart-of-the-home renovations to expansive commercial environments, we deliver premium installations.

Full Kitchen Installations

Complete dry-fit and wet-fit installations. We expertly assemble and install cabinetry, ensuring perfectly level base units, seamlessly scribed cornices, and flawlessly aligned fascias.

Precision Worktop Fitting

Masterful cutting and joining of solid wood, premium laminate, and composite worktops. We utilize specialized jigs to ensure invisible masons mitre joints and perfectly routed sink cut-outs.

Multi-Trade Coordination

We handle the plumbing, electrical, and carpentry. Our network of certified tradesmen will safely install gas appliances, rewire lighting circuits, and connect sinks and dishwashers.

Retail & Office Fit-Outs

Applying our domestic precision to the commercial sector. We fit bespoke reception desks, staff kitchenettes, and custom retail shelving systems designed for heavy traffic.

Appliance Integration

Flawless integration of built-in appliances. From sliding hinge fridge-freezers to flush-mounted induction hobs and downdraft extractors, we ensure a streamlined, modern aesthetic.

Rip-Out & Prep

A great finish starts with a blank canvas. We handle the careful dismantling and responsible disposal of your old kitchen, followed by re-plastering and floor levelling as required.
